RWST: Rural Water and Sanitation Team

Established in 2017 by a team of WASH professionals, RWST is a social enterprise dedicated to improving water, sanitation, and hygiene services in rural Cambodia. We partner with water associations, NGOs, and private sector stakeholders to enhance water business operations and service quality.

Our vision is that all Cambodian people in rural areas have access to sustainable and resilient clean and safe water supply and sanitation services.

Our Services

Comprehensive WASH solutions from capacity building to technical implementation

WASH Capacity Building

Training, coaching, and mentoring for WASH actors including local officers, NGOs, and community-based organizations.

  • Online Training Series (6/year)
  • WASH Webinar Series
  • CAM-WASH Knowledge Platform

Research & Evaluation

Comprehensive research studies and project evaluations for development partners, NGOs, and private companies.

  • Project Impact Evaluation
  • Market Surveys
  • WASH Workforce Scoping

Technical Consultancy

Water engineering services including feasibility studies, water treatment plant design, and infrastructure assessment.

  • Water Treatment Design
  • Pipe Network Planning
  • Construction Supervision

Water Supply Solutions

Provision of clean, safe, and affordable drinking water systems for schools, health centers, and communities.

  • UV & UF Filter Systems
  • Bio-sand Filters
  • Arsenic Removal Units

WASH Agent Program

Franchise model supporting local entrepreneurs to become one-stop-shops for WASH services in their communities.

  • Information Center
  • Product Distribution
  • Service Connection

Business Development

Support for water SMEs including financial modeling, business planning, and access to finance.

  • Feasibility Studies
  • Loan Application Support
  • Financial Modeling
